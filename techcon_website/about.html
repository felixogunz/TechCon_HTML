Origins and Evolution of TechCon

TechCon was started in 2011 with the sole aim of motivating aspiring entrepreneurs to fulfil their tech goals. It was also created out of a need to create job opportunities for the ever growing youths.

Mission of TechCon Conference

It's Mission is to empower 50 million African Tech entrepreneur before the end of 2030.
